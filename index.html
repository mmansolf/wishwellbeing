Welcome to WISH

We are a consortium dedicated to advancing the science and practice of healthcare worker well-being improvement. Our multiprofessional team develops rigorous measurement tools and evidence-based frameworks to drive meaningful, systems-level change. At the heart of our work is the belief that sustainable, fulfilling careers in healthcare are possible by improving our healthcare systems.

The cornerstone of our efforts is the Well-Being Influencers Survey for Healthcare (WISH)—a validated survey tool that quantifies the complex construct of healthcare worker well-being through a systems lens. By assessing and highlighting the factors that shape workplace experience, we help clarify what meaningful well-being prioritization looks like—not just for clinicians, but for the long-term stability of healthcare organizations and the quality of patient care.
